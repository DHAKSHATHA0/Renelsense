Clinical Innovation in Kidney Monitoring

Real-time kidney function assessment using advanced bioimpedance and optical sensing

The Challenge

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) affects 850+ million people globally. Early detection is critical for preventing progression to kidney failure, yet most cases go undiagnosed until late stages due to:

  • Limited access to laboratory testing
  • Inability for continuous home monitoring
  • Delayed diagnosis by 5-10 years on average
  • No real-time disease progression tracking

Our Innovation

The first non-invasive, real-time kidney function monitoring system that uses advanced sensing and AI to predict eGFR within seconds:

  • Multi-frequency bioimpedance spectroscopy (1kHz - 200kHz)
  • Optical photoplethysmography sensors
  • Machine learning eGFR prediction (<95% accuracy)
  • Real-time visualization and clinical insights
